English Version
1. Introduction
Gym Timer ("the App") is developed by Crasia. This privacy policy explains how your personal data is handled when you use the application.

2. Data Collection
Gym Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device and all data is stored locally.

Data Used by the App:
Set and rest durations: Stored locally on your device only
Theme preferences: Stored locally on your device only
Sound and vibration settings: Stored locally on your device only
Last workout information: Stored locally on your device only
3. Data Sharing
The app does not share any data with third parties. It does not require an internet connection and does not send any data to any servers.

4. Permissions
The app uses the following permissions:

Vibration (VIBRATE): Used to send vibration notifications when the timer ends
This permission is only used for the stated purpose and no data is collected.

5. Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security depends on your device's security. No data is transferred to the internet or external servers.

6. Children's Privacy
Since our app does not collect any personal data, it can be safely used by users of all ages.

7. Changes
This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified of significant changes through the app or this page.

8. Contact
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us:

Email: crasia.apps@gmail.com
